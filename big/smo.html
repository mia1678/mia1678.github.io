Social Media Optimization (SMO) Research

Social Media Optimization is strategically using social medias and communities to increase the online presence of their company, services, products, brand, or events. For instance, many companies tend to create accounts on social medias such as Instagram, Facebook, and Twitter in order to be in touch with their target customers (Jackson).

SMO allows corporations to strengthen their brands, generate leads, get more online presence, and to connect with their audiences. SMO and SEO are both fairly important, based on a survey form Search Engine Land, it indicates that 50% of the companies that did not succeed in SEO did not integrate with their social media marketing (Jackson). In addition, according to the MIT Sloan Management Review/Deloitte University Press social business study, 28% of the organizations think the lack of strategies of social media is the top barrier for them to becoming the social business (Kiron, David).

SMO is important because it provides the business a place for marketing and optimization of business. It is about sharing information and content to the businesses’ target customers and to attract those people to purchase the product from the businesses. SMO is an important factor in search engine optimization, which is a part of the process of designing a website do the businesses can show up on a higher ranking on the search engine which is also related to keyboard research. If the company know what topics, keywords, hashtags, their target customers will be searching on, they will be able to provide some other relevant contents for them. Moreover, Optimizing companies’ profiles and web page are necessary as well so that the search engine can understand what the website is about in order for the search engine direct those target customers to companies’ web page. Businesses can optimize their profiles by completing those sites like Facebook, Twitter and Linkedin that allows people to fill in the complete and detail information that allows search engine to find. Furthermore, adding information like bio, username, profile picture and logo can also optimize the companies’ profile in order to let the companies’ websites stand out (Jackson).
