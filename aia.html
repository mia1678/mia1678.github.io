Audience Issue Approach

Prospective Clients

These are businesses, and the users are probably middle aged, middle class and well-educated who know basic skills of using computers. They mostly use IE or Chrome browsers on the laptop or desktop computers in their office.
  • what services the company provide
  • what products the company produce
  • contact information and how to reach them
  • A page for explaining what the company produce and the introduction for the product
  • A page that includes a contact from in order to let prospective clients to reach the company

Affiliates

The corporations and businesses that mainly use IE or Chrome browsers on the laptop or desktop computers. The user are usually in middle aged and well-educated and know skills to function computers.
  • what services the company provide
  • detail information of products the company produces
  • contact information and how to reach them
  • the information of the factory
  • past experience of cooperating with other businesses
  • history of the company
  • A page for explaining what the company produce and the detail information for the products as well as the information of the factory.
  • A page that includes a contact from in order to let people reach the company.
  • A page that includes the history, the introduction of the company, and the past experience of cooperating with other companies.

Prospective Employees

These are people who are looking for job opportunities in this company, who know how to function computer properly. They usually use IE, Safari, and Chrome on laptop, desktop computer or mobile devices. They are in middle aged with fairly equal distribution of gender.
  • what services the company provide
  • what products the company produce
  • contact information and how to reach them
  • background and history of the company
  • A page for explaining what services and products the company produces
  • A page that includes a contact from in order to let people reach the company.
  • A page that includes the history, the introduction of the company, and the past experience of cooperating with other companies.
